# Corporate Memphis

Corporate Memphis (also known as Alegria) is a flat geometric art style widely used in Big Tech illustrations. It is designed to look friendly, inclusive, and scalable.

## Design Tokens

### Colors
| Token | Value | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `--bg-color` | `#f2f6ff` | Light Background |
| `--primary` | `#5850ec` | Corporate Blue |
| `--secondary` | `#ffadad` | Soft Pink |
| `--text-color` | `#2d3748` | Dark Grey Text |

### Principles
*   **Flatness**: No gradients or textures in illustrations.
*   **Geometry**: Use simple shapes (circles, squares) to construct complex scenes.
*   **Softness**: Rounded corners (`border-radius: 16px+`) and soft shadows.

.corp-card {
