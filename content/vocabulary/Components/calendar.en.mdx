> A component for displaying dates and scheduling events.

## Usage

Calendars are used to visualize data over time, schedule events, and manage timelines. Common use cases include:

- Event management systems
- Booking interfaces
- Project timelines
- Personal organizers

## Examples

### Month View

```html
<div class="calendar-month">
  <div class="header">
    <button>Prev</button>
    <h2>July 2024</h2>
    <button>Next</button>
  </div>
  <div class="days-grid">
    <div class="day-name">Sun</div>
    <!-- ... other day names ... -->
    <div class="day">1</div>
    <div class="day">2</div>
    <!-- ... other days ... -->
    <div class="day has-event">15</div>
  </div>
</div>
```

## Best Practices

- **Navigation**: Provide easy controls to switch between months, weeks, or years.
- **Indicators**: Clearly mark current date and days with events.
- **Detail View**: Allow users to click on a day or event to see more details.
