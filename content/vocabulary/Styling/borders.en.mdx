> **Borders** are lines that surround the content and padding of an element.

## Syntax
```css
/* Shorthand: width style color */
border: 1px solid black;

/* Individual properties */
border-width: 2px;
border-style: dashed;
border-color: red;
```

## Common Styles
-   `solid`: A single solid line.
-   `dashed`: A series of short square-ended dashes.
-   `dotted`: A series of round dots.
-   `double`: Two parallel solid lines.
-   `none`: No border.

## Use Cases
-   Defining the edges of cards or buttons.
-   Creating separators between elements.
-   Highlighting active or focused elements.
