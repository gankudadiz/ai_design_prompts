> **Gradients** let you display smooth transitions between two or more specified colors.

## Types
-   **Linear Gradient** (`linear-gradient()`): Colors transition along a straight line (down, up, left, right, or diagonally).
-   **Radial Gradient** (`radial-gradient()`): Colors radiate from a central point.
-   **Conic Gradient** (`conic-gradient()`): Colors transition around a center point (like a color wheel).

## Syntax
```css
background: linear-gradient(90deg, red, blue);
background: radial-gradient(circle, red, blue);
background: conic-gradient(red, blue, green);
```

## Use Cases
-   Backgrounds for buttons or sections.
-   Creating metallic or glossy effects.
-   Progress bars or pie charts (using conic gradients).
